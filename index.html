ABOUT ME

I'm Niah Ferlito, an Illustrator from the PNW and recent grad from The Evergreen State College. I make weird stuff about normal stuff, but mostly comics and zines.

I make art that is experimental in its presentation, thought provoking in its impact, and emotionaly relatable at its core. Through my writing I try to encourage people to be creative, and enable them to do so sustainably in a way that is focused on self reflection, personal improvement, and honest expression. Most importantly I want to use my creativity and influence to affect progresive social change, and encourage others to do the same through their interests.
